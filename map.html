Find the Halifax Gibbet

Location


The Halifax Gibbet is located in Halifax, West Yorkshire, England. The exact location of the original gibbet is near the Gibbet Street area, close to the town center.


For visitors interested in seeing the site, it is recommended to explore the historical landmarks in Halifax, as there may not be a specific marker for the gibbet itself.


Halifax is a town with a rich history, and visitors can also explore other historical sites and museums in the area to learn more about its past.
